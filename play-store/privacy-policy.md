# Privacy Policy for HwpViewer

Effective date: May 5, 2026

HwpViewer is a document viewer for opening HWP and HWPX files on Android.

## Information We Process

HwpViewer may process document files that you choose to open or share with the app. These files may include HWP or HWPX documents and their file names.

The app does not require account registration and does not ask you to provide a name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, camera, microphone, or payment information.

## How Documents Are Used

HwpViewer uses selected documents to display their contents in the app.

If PDF conversion is enabled, the selected document may be sent to a configured HTTPS document conversion server so that the app can display a converted PDF version. The document is used only to provide this conversion and viewing functionality.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not share documents with advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

If document conversion is enabled, documents are transmitted only to the configured conversion server for app functionality.

## Data Retention

HwpViewer does not create user accounts or long-term user profiles.

Documents opened locally remain on your device unless you choose to store or share them elsewhere.

If a conversion server is used, uploaded documents should be deleted after conversion. Converted temporary output should be retained only as long as needed to provide the viewing feature and should be cleaned up regularly by the server operator.

## Security

Release builds of HwpViewer require HTTPS for document conversion traffic. Debug builds may use local development HTTP endpoints for testing only.

The app does not request broad external storage permissions in release builds. It receives access only to files that you open or share with the app through Android file sharing or document handling.

## Children's Privacy

HwpViewer is a general document utility and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time. You can remove the app from your device through Android settings.

If you use a hosted conversion server and need deletion of a document processed by that server, contact the developer or server operator listed on the Google Play store listing.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer email listed on the Google Play store listing.
